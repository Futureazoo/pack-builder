About Me

I've been playing Minecraft since 2012, and since the start I've loved making textures for the game. I loved being able to see my own drawings making up the world around me, and making sweeping changes with relatively low effort. Even when I got burnt out on the game, I would still make textures and models for fun. Minecraft was my first real exposure to pixel art, and it encouraged me to experiment with new forms and styles of digital drawing. Even though I don't play it as much as I used to, Minecraft will always have a special place in my heart, and be a core part of my childhood.
